comment: Death spiral. Even though it has a small share of overall retail sales, Amazon drives down prices and ups convenience. Retailers can’t compete, and some go bankrupt. This creates uncertainty that inhibits hiring, which further undermines service, giving more advantage to Amazon. And the spiral continues.
