* Information technology jobs are equally susceptible to automation if they involve repetitive tasks that are within the grasp of increasingly sophisticated machines. For example, Morgan Stanley calculates the salary of a regulated financial institution IT operations worker in New York at $70-80,000 while an annual license for a machine that can do five of their jobs would cost just $8-11,000.[^1]
