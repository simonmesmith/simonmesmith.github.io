* Information technology jobs are equally susceptible to automation if they involve repetitive tasks that are within the grasp of increasingly sophisticated machines.
* Morgan Stanley calculates the salary of a regulated financial institution IT operations worker in New York at $70-80,000 while an annual license for a machine that can do five of their jobs would cost just $8-11,000.[^1]
* Indian IT consulting firms are cutting jobs due to IT automation. Infosys decreased hiring 65% in 2016 over the prior year, for example, and Tata Consultancy Services decreased hiring in the same time period by 10,000— and says future hiring will be even lower.[^2]
