* Researchers working on AI predict that by 2045, AI will be editing film, filling in gaps in footage and creating believable live-action characters.[^1]

* A product called [Lyrebird](https://lyrebird.ai/) allows you to train an AI on your voice and then generate your own speech from text; since it can do the same with any voice, it may pose a significant threat to voiceover artists and voice actors (or a new opportunity to scale their work).[^2]

[^1]:	['Digital humans' created by AI will dominate Hollywood by 2045](http://ift.tt/2vosc3W)

[^2]:	[Lyrebird Beta — Create your own digital voice using AI](http://ift.tt/2oXDSu4)
