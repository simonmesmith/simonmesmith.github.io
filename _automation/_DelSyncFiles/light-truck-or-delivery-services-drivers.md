* Light deliveries (think most Amazon packages) are well on their way to being automated. While regulatory restrictions have kept advances well-hidden from those in the US, countries with more permissive drone policies are moving forward rapidly with experiments. Matternet, for example, is testing autonomous delivery drones outside the US that can refuel and reload by themselves.[^1]
