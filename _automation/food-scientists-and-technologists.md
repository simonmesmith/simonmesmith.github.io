* Brewing beer takes intuition. At London-based IntelligentX Brewing Co., this intuition is informed by machine-learning algorithms that automatically analyze customer feedback on their beers.[^1]
* Coca Cola is using AI to develop new flavors. For example, it monitors data from self-service soft drink fountains that allow customers to mix their own drinks. It used such analysis to decide on launching Cherry Sprite.[^2]
