* Recruiting and hiring talent can be tremendously time-consuming for companies, and gets harder as they grow and need to fill more—and, often, more specific—positions. One solution that uses AI to help is HireVue, a "video interview intelligence platform" that can analyze applicants' faces to tell companies whether they're a good hire.[^1] Because it can screen many candidates rapidly, HireVue could reduce some of the burden on—as well as some of the need for—hiring managers and recruiters.
