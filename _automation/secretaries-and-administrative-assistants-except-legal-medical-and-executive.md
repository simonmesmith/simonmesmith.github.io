* AI has now achieved human parity in speech recognition[^1], meaning—when you factor cost-savings alone—transcribing is a job best done by machines. In addition, since understanding speech is key to expanding the capabilities of a growing number of virtual assistants such as Siri, Alexa and Cortana, this development likely heralds the beginning of the end for human assistants.

[^1]:	[Achieving Human Parity in Conversational Speech Recognition](https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.05256)
