* As a vegetarian, my preference would be to eliminate animal slaughter completely and move to an entirely plant-based diet supplemented by lab-grown meat for those who really can't live without animal products. But until that happens, many people work in meat slaughtering and packing—and their jobs are slowly disappearing.

* Between 2011 and 2016,  thanks to automation, there was an 8.4% decline in  slaughterers and meat packers while there was a corresponding 6.15% increase in production between 2015 and 2016 alone. [^1] These high-level stats hide the fact that there was a decline in unskilled "knife work" (yuck) and slow growth in more skilled work. So the automation of low-skilled work is much greater than the 8.4% figure suggests.

[^1]:	[How the Meat Industry Thinks About Non-Meat-Eaters](http://ift.tt/2xAiey3)
