*  As of 2015, truck driver was the most common job in 29 of the 50 US states.[^1]
* In 2016, a White House report predicted that 2.2 to 3.1 million US jobs would be threatened or significantly altered by autonomous vehicle technology, and that truck driving would be among them.[^2]
* Tesla is working on an autonomous semi-trailer truck, as are most major truck makers.[^3]
