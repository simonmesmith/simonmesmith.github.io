* [Logojoy](https://logojoy.com/) uses artificial intelligence to create logos. As of mid-September 2017, they report creating more than 8.5 million logos.

* Whether you use an automated logo maker or a human designer, you can leverage AI to _critique_ your logo. [Logo Rank](http://brandmark.io/logo-rank/) was trained to do so using more than a million logos.
