* It might seem unlikely that art directors, creative directors and other creative professionals could be automated, since creative professions are supposed to be more immune to the impact of artificial intelligence. But there are signs that aspects of the creative process, if not the entire process, can indeed be automated. 
* In March 2017, advertising agency McCann announced creating an AI creative director. Despite seeming to be more novelty than technological breakthrough, it directed an ad that was deemed superior to a competing human-directed version by advertising executives.[^1]
* One of the world’s biggest advertisers, Coca Cola, is reportedly investigating the use of artificial intelligence to create advertisements.[^2]
