* Vocal tone and intonation can reveal a lot of information that a well-trained neural network can decode. Researchers have used this capability to successfully predict, based on voice analysis alone, which couples will break up. “Predictions of relationship outcomes obtained directly from vocal acoustics are comparable or superior to those obtained using human-rated behavioral codes as prediction features,” the researchers report.[^1]
