* Think you wouldn't watch a movie made by an AI or featuring AI actors? Given how much computer-generated imagery now features in top-grossing films, you might not even know. And such computerization should only increase. 

* Researchers involved with AI in film, for example, predict that by 2045 AI will be editing films, filling gaps in footage and creating believable live-action characters.[^1] (I think this is an incredibly conservative estimate, and my gut says the technology will be available within five years and—if it offers sufficient advantages, including economic—widely deployed within 10.)
